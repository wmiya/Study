About

Safety, security and efficiency are top requirements in maritime operations both onboard and onshore. Accurate and reliable environmental measurements and monitoring are vital for ensuring safe and efficient operations in ports, ships and offshore platforms.

Offshore Weather Measurements

By having reliable and accurate real-time weather observations of your working environment you won't have to make your operational decisions based on intuition. The decision will have consequences from many aspects usually balancing between safety and economic efficiency.

Our company can provide you with reliable weather observations measured by our unique technology, continuously developed by our professional team of scientists and engineers.

Ports, Coast and Waterways

Building on almost 80 years of experience, Vaisala has a decades-long tradition in serving customers in more than 120 countries. Unique understanding of weather measurement, long experience and global presence has made Vaisala the preferred weather solution supplier to many Vessel Traffic Service (VTS) organizations and system integrators. Our reference deliveries range from meteo/hydro sensors for navigation aids and automatic weather stations for VTS systems to turnkey network weather solutions for Coastal Surveillance System (CSS), which consist of a large number of automatic weather stations with sensors, telemetry, network data collection, management and visualization software, installation and maintenance services.​​
